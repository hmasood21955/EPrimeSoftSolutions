Project New York

We have developed a comprehensive web application that caters to the needs of pet owners, doctors, and admins.

PetMediConnect brings together various functionalities to ensure the best care for pets and a seamless experience for users.

Key Features:
  • Online Pet Checkups & Appointments: Users can easily book appointments with vets, including online consultations via Zoom.
  • Pet Store: A wide range of products like pet food, accessories, and medicines, all available online with easy payments through Stripe.
  • Community Forum: A platform where users can ask questions, share experiences, and connect with other pet owners.
  • Chatbot Integration: For quick answers to general queries about the app and its features.
  • Email Service: Automated emails for appointment confirmations and updates.
  • Twilio Message Service: SMS notifications for appointment reminders and confirmations.
  • Zoom API Integration: Facilitating online consultations directly through the app.
  • Stripe for Payment: Secure and seamless payment processing for consultations and store purchases.
Roles:
  • Admin: Manages doctors' registrations, appointments, store products, and payment approvals.
  • Doctor: Manages their profile, appointments, and approves patient consultations.
  • User/Patient: Registers, books appointments, makes payments, purchases products, and engages in the community forum.

Technologies Used: MERN (MongoDB, Express, React, Node.js)
